Intro to Bonsai

Bonsai is an art form using miniature trees. Many people don't know the nature of Bonsai trees. Did you know Bonsai, the practice of keeping a tree in miniature form, was practiced in Japan and China for centuries? In this informative site, you can learn all about what Bonsai is and how to care for one of your own.
